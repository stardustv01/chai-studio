# Chai Studio — Brand concepts V4

This round explores one first-principles identity: **The Warm Timeline**.

The Devanagari word `चाय` carries the emotional identity. Its headline becomes an editing timeline; a cyan playhead represents precision; an amber inner gesture represents warmth, freshness, and creative thought.

## Directions

1. `01-calligraphic-warm-timeline.png` — the most expressive direction. Memorable, but the rising gesture reads too literally as steam and should not be retained.
2. `02-modern-geometric.png` — the strongest compact silhouette. It performs best at small UI sizes but feels more like a generic software system than Chai.
3. `03-balanced-hybrid.png` — the strongest overall concept. It keeps the warm gesture inside the Devanagari structure and pairs it with an exact editorial playhead.

`00-context-test.png` places all three generated symbol crops into the same 48px header, project-card, and Dock contexts. `04-motion-strip.png` records the proposed three-beat logo sting: timeline draws, playhead lands, amber pulse settles.

## Honest status

These are ImageGen concept rasters, not production logo masters. They are suitable for choosing a direction only. Generated lettering must not be traced blindly: the approved direction should be redrawn as deterministic SVG, checked by a fluent Devanagari reader, optically corrected at 16/24/32/48px, and exported in full-color, one-color, dark, light, and reduced-motion variants.

The current recommendation is to develop direction 03 using direction 02's small-size discipline. Do not ship direction 01's rising plume.

## Motion behavior to build after approval

- 0–220ms: the Devanagari headline/timeline draws left to right.
- 180–360ms: the cyan playhead arrives and settles without bounce.
- 300–620ms: the amber inner pulse reveals once and rests.
- Total duration: approximately 620ms; no loop in ordinary UI.
- Reduced motion: instant final mark with a short opacity fade only.

The live Chai Studio UI has not been modified in this exploration.
